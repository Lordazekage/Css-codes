A QUICK POEM BY YOURS TRULY

Cupid,
With thy childish innocence_
And thy mischief, thou cause_
A stranger to adore another,
His heart bleeding; from a spell_
Shot from thy bowstring.
Thou made antonyms into synonyms,
Black becomes white and white is black.
Two is one, fayed together eternally.
Valiant; thou hath yoked hatred's spawn.
Clothed not, but with thy piety for Venus,
Thou art a true patron of her domain.
A knight of honour, our prayers ye desert
For thou art, that which makes love remain.
By: Badmus Aze Leon
